ChangeCurDir

Key: Shift+Ctrl+D
Menu: Toggles > Cursor direction

Change the direction the cursor moves after typing a character or backspace. The direction can be selected by any of the arrow keys; Enter will restore the normal direction.

When the cursor is not updating the usual way, overwrite mode is always on and the new direction is indicated in the mode line. BackSpace will move in the opposite direction and overwrite the character with a space.
