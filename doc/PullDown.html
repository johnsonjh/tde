PullDown

Key: Ctrl+\
Menu: n/a

Bring up a pop-up pull-down menu, containing most of the editor functions. Even though it is not displayed, the headings can be selected by number (the left-most is `1'). The menu and the names given to the keys are configurable — see config/menu.cfg and the end of config/tde.cfg for usage.
