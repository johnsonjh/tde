ToggleTabInflate

Key: Shift+Alt+T
Menu: Word > Tabs > Inflate tabs

Select one of three tab modes: deflate tabs treats tabs as normal characters; inflate tabs expands tabs to spaces when editing, then compresses spaces to tabs when done; real tabs edits with the tab characters, when possible. Spaces are not compressed after a quote character (either `"' or `''). The tab mode is separate for each file.
